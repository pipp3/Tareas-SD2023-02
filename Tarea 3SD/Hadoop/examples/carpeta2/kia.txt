21<splittername>"K\u0418 Corporation, formally known as Kia (Korean: \uae30\uc544, IPA: [kn.com], formerly known as Kyungsung Precision Industry (\u4eac\u57ce\u7cbe\u5bc6\u5de5\u696d) and Kia Motors Corporation, is a South Korean multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Seoul, South Korea. It is South Korea's second largest automobile manufacturer, after its parent company, Hyundai Motor Company, with sales of over 2.8 million vehicles in 2019. As of 2015, the K\u0418 (Kia) Corporation is minority owned by Hondai (Hyundai), which holds a 33.88% stake valued at just over US$6 billion. K\u0418 (Kia) in turn is a minority owner of more than twenty Hyundai subsidiaries ranging from 4.9% up to 45.37%, totaling more than US$8.3 billion."
